Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy for College Placement System

We value your trust and are committed to protecting your personal and academic information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your data when using the College Placement Portal.

1. Information We Collect
  • Personal Details (Name, Phone, Email, Gender, Address)
  • Academic Details (CGPA, Branch, Year, Backlogs)
  • Schooling Background (10th %, 12th %)
  • Placement Activity (Companies Applied, Status, Selected Category)
  • Login Credentials (securely stored using browser localStorage)
2. Why We Collect This Information
  • To determine eligibility for various recruitment drives
  • To personalize your dashboard experience
  • To maintain placement transparency and accuracy
  • To send alerts regarding results, deadlines, and updates
  • To assist placement officers in decision making
3. Data Protection & Security

Your information is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. No data is sent to external servers. We ensure:

  • No third-party data sharing
  • No cookies or external trackers used
  • Secure client-side storage
4. Your Rights
  • Right to edit or update your data anytime
  • Right to delete your account/profile completely
  • Right to opt out from placement drives
  • Right to request policy details or clarifications
5. Data Retention

Your data remains stored on your device unless you clear your browser storage or delete your account using the profile section.

6. Children’s Privacy

This platform is intended only for college students above 17 years of age. No data is knowingly collected from minors.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will appear on this page. Continued usage of the portal means you accept the updated policy.
